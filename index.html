Trackit

Effortlessly track life your way. Trackit is completely customizable and authentically social.

Trackit is the easiest way to track anything in your life. Just speak, and let the app automatically track what happened in your day into categories you design. These fully customizable categories let you track anything: fitness goals, journaling, meals, moods, hobbies, habits, and more. Add photos, voice memos, and any custom data to your categories and entries to keep track of what really matters to you, in any level of detail you want! With Trackit you can track privately, join groups to keep you accountable, or share with friends to stay authentically connected. Trackit makes capturing your life natural, easy, and meaningful, so you never lose track of what matters most.
